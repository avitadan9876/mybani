The Story of Two Birds

Like water reflects the face, so one’s life reflects the heart.

Two birds were born from the same nest, carried by the same wind. Though they looked alike, each had a different song within. One flew freely, curious and bright, while the other lingered, cautious, quietly watching. One day, their paths crossed mid-air, and for the first time, they saw in each other a reflection not just of wings or feathers, but of longing, love, and the sky between.

And so they flew, not one after the other, but side by side.

— For Avital, always reflected in the calmest skies.
